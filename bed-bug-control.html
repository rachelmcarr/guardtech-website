Our Primary Method

Why We Use Heat Instead of Chemicals

Bed bugs hide deep in mattress seams, box springs, baseboards, outlets, and furniture joints — places a chemical spray often can't fully reach, and where surviving eggs can reinfest a home even after treatment. Heat treatment solves this by raising the temperature of the room (or your entire home) to a level that's lethal to bed bugs and their eggs at every life stage, all in a single visit.

  • ✓
    Kills every life stage

    Unlike sprays, heat reaches eggs as well as adults and juveniles, so there's no lingering hatch-out after treatment.

  • ✓
    No chemical residue

    Nothing to air out or wipe down, and no need to keep kids or pets out of a treated room afterward.

  • ✓
    Reaches hiding spots sprays miss

    Heat penetrates mattress seams, wall voids, outlets, and furniture joints a spray can't fully cover.
