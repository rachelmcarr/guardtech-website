Our Approach

We Stop Pests Before They Ever Get Inside

Most pest control starts indoors, after there's already a problem. We do it differently: our technicians build a treated barrier around the outside of your home — entry points, foundation, eaves, and yard — so pests are intercepted before they cross the threshold.

  • ✓
    Less chemical exposure indoors

    Treatment stays outside, where your family and pets already aren't.

  • ✓
    Faster, easier appointments

    No need to clear rooms or be home — most visits happen entirely outside.

  • ✓
    Interior treatment always available

    Want it anyway? It's included on request at no extra cost — just usually not necessary.
