What Is a WDIR?

Wood Destroying Insect Report

A WDIR documents evidence of termites and other wood-destroying insects found during a licensed inspection of a property. Lenders, real estate agents, and closing attorneys frequently require this report as part of the sale process.

1

Licensed Inspection

A certified inspector examines accessible areas of the home.

2

Fast Turnaround

Reports typically completed within days to meet closing timelines.

3

Clear Documentation

Standardized report accepted by lenders, agents, and title companies.

4

Follow-Up Treatment

If issues are found, we can provide treatment options and next steps.
