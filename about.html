Our Story

How We Got Started

GuardTech Pest Management traces back to 1999, when Kenneth Carr and his brother were running a home inspection company in Beaumont, Texas. Every time a termite inspection came up, they had to bring in an outside contractor to get it done. After enough of those calls, the answer seemed obvious: why not just do it themselves? That question turned into HomeGuard Pest Control — the company that would eventually grow into GuardTech Pest Management.

More than two decades later, that same instinct is still what drives us: if something needs doing right, do it yourself and do right by the people you're doing it for. GuardTech remains 100% locally owned and family operated. Every technician who steps into your yard is trained, background-checked, and treats your property like it's their own.
