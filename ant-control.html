Crazy Ants

Fast-Moving, Fast-Spreading

Crazy ants don't build visible mounds like fire ants — instead they nest in huge numbers under debris, mulch, and even inside electrical equipment, where they're known to cause damage. Treatment requires a different approach than standard ant control, and we tailor it to where the colony is actually nesting.
